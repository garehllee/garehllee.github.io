Endangered Animal Illustration



TIMELINE: 3 weeks | APPLICATIONS: Adobe Illustrator, EZ Gif Maker

We were asked to create a poster and gif in Illustrator based on an endangered animal from somewhere we have lived. Compositions are constrained to six colors and one typeface.

The presentation for this project might look slightly different than other pages. Google slides were used to display process this semester, and since this is a "play" project, I simply compiled them here.
